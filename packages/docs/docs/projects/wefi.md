## Description
WeFi positions itself as building "a financial system that gives people the tools of a bank without needing a bank." Key features:
- Self-custody + programmable payments + crypto-to-fiat spending
- Focus on emerging markets where traditional banking is limited
- AI-driven compliance + ZK compliance layers
- Hybrid custodial structure on blockchain rails
- WFI token for fees, rewards, and governance

Target markets are regions with expensive remittances, limited banking access, and mobile-first populations.

6. **Regulatory Compliance** - Publish licensing status and regulatory approach
7. **Partnership Announcements** - Secure and announce strategic partnerships
8. **Tokenomics Documentation** - Release detailed tokenomics paper

## Verdict
WeFi presents an interesting concept addressing legitimate market needs (emerging market financial services, remittances), but the complete lack of team transparency, zero updates, and minimal community engagement are severe red flags. The project appears to be either very early stage, abandoned, or potentially not legitimate.

The technical concepts mentioned (AI compliance, ZK proofs, hybrid custody) are valid approaches, but without any verifiable team or product evidence, investment carries extreme risk.

**Recommendation:** Avoid until team is disclosed and working product is demonstrated. The concept is sound but execution evidence is completely absent.
