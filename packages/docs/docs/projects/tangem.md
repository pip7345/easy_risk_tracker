## üìù Verdict

**Risk Tier: üü¢ Low Risk (7.8/10)**

Tangem is a legitimate, established hardware wallet company - not a DePIN or token project. The Swiss jurisdiction, SBI backing, 7+ year track record, and global distribution make it a credible security product option.

**Suitable For:**
- Users seeking hardware wallet for crypto storage
- Those who value portability (smartcard form factor)
- Users wanting mobile-friendly NFC interaction
- People who prefer established companies over startups

**Not Suitable For:**
- Those seeking passive income or token earnings
- Users requiring open-source firmware
- Speculators looking for investment returns
- Those needing extensive on-device features (limited by form factor)

**Recommendation:** Tangem is a legitimate product choice for cryptocurrency security, not an earning opportunity. Evaluate based on your security needs, preferred form factor, and comparison with other hardware wallet options (Ledger, Trezor). The smartcard format offers unique advantages for portability and everyday carry.
