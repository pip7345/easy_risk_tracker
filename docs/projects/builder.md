## Project Description

Builder is a Polygon-based decentralized staking protocol with an elastic supply that expands through scheduled minting tied to new staking activity. The ecosystem positions its long-term vision around real-estate and mortgage-related use cases, where future applications may leverage the Builder community and staking system as a financial layer. Participants earn weekly rewards with principal released over long, calendar-based lockups (200 weeks), alongside referral and ambassador incentives. The protocol operates without a centralized company, with rewards driven primarily by protocol emissions rather than active mortgage cash flows.
