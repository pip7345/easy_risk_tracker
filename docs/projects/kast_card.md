## Project Description

Kast Card is a fintech/crypto-card product that allows users to spend stablecoins (USDC, USDT, PYUSD) directly via Visa cards (physical and virtual). The platform offers global USD-stable accounts, multiple currency/stablecoin deposits, Apple Pay/Google Pay integration, and availability in 160+ countries. Users earn "KAST Points" for spending, referrals, and usage, with bonus multipliers for staking SOL. The product combines traditional payment rails with crypto assets.
