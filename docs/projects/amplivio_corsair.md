## Description

Amplivo is a community-driven platform that empowers individuals to participate in a mission to reduce plastic waste while earning through its referral-based model. Corsair Group International positions itself as the industrial engine behind that mission, claiming to convert plastic waste into usable fuel through pyrolysis technology. Together, they present a narrative where community engagement fuels environmental impact and tokenized value creation. They promote a circular economy where collective action and innovative processing create both environmental and financial benefits.

**Admins/Leadership:** Patty Edgecombe, Andre' Normann, CEO Jussi Saloranta
