## üìù Verdict

**Risk Tier: üü° Moderate (6.4/10)**

Gradient appears to be a legitimate DePIN project with real technical development, academic research backing, and strong VC funding. The pre-token status introduces uncertainty, but the fundamentals suggest genuine product development rather than a cash-grab scheme.

**Suitable For:**
- Tech-savvy users interested in early DePIN participation
- Those willing to contribute compute resources for potential future rewards
- Users comfortable with pre-token uncertainty

**Not Suitable For:**
- Those seeking immediate returns or guaranteed rewards
- Risk-averse investors requiring proven tokenomics
- Users expecting passive income without resource contribution

**Recommendation:** Consider participating in Sentry Node program with the understanding that rewards are speculative and depend on future token launch and distribution. Monitor tokenomics announcements closely before any financial commitment.
