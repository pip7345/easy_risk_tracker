## Project Description

Blockchain Sports Ecosystem positions itself as a multi-product sports and Web3 platform including academies, scouting, NFT athlete cards, and analytics. The public site contains a detailed roadmap, team/advisors list, and multiple subprojects. The roadmap emphasizes football academies, AI analytics, NFT marketplaces, and integration with the Atleta blockchain stack.
