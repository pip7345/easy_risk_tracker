My Business Club (MBC) is a "private membership platform" for entrepreneurs. Key features include "Smart Contract Pay" for instant payouts, global profit sharing, educational content, podcasts, and travel perks. The platform emphasizes "no delays, no middlemen" with payments going directly to wallets.
